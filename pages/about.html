Who We Are

about us

PowerHub Consultants Ltd is a private limited company registered under the Companies Act Cap 486 of the Laws of Kenya. It started operations in 2017 and was incorporated in mid-2019.The company was founded to align itself with the efforts towards the achievement of Kenya’s Vision 2030, an economic blueprint which envisages Kenya to be a middle-income nation by the year 2030. Our main premise is harnessing useful human resource in the field of electrical and mechanical engineering to provide professional and reliable solutions in the booming infrastructure sector. With that persuasion, the company has been making inroads in the electromechanical and construction sectors, which are inarguably the strongest pillars on which the modernization of an economy draws its strength from. During the course of our operations, we’ve established a strong clientele portfolio that ranges from private residences, to commercial and industrial premises. The continuity of service with these clients is an attestation of the top-notch quality of our workmanship. Our project handling capacity is modest, in the range of Kshs 50million, but growing thanks to our expanding sphere of influence which is now covering a majority of the urban centers in Kenya
