About Us

Front End Developer

PowerHub Consultants Ltd is a private limited company registered under the Companies Act Cap 486 of the Laws of Kenya. It started operations in 2017 and was incorporated in mid-2019. Our main premise is harnessing useful human resource in the field of electrical and mechanical engineering to provide professional and reliable solutions in the booming infrastructure sector. With that persuasion, the company has been making inroads in the electromechanical and construction sectors, which are inarguably the strongest pillars on which the modernization of an economy draws its strength from.
